# Customer Experience Enhancement: Future Features Roadmap

To elevate Bexiemart from a great app to an elite, world-class e-commerce platform, we should consider implementing the following features. Each suggestion is designed to reduce friction, build trust, and increase customer retention.

---

## 1. Real-Time Delivery Tracking (Live Map)
**The Feature:** A live, interactive map tracking the exact location of the delivery rider, similar to Uber or Glovo.
**The Flow:** 
- The user receives a push notification: *"Your order is on the way!"*
- Tapping the notification (or clicking "Track" in the Orders screen) opens a full-screen map.
- The map displays the user's destination pin and a moving vehicle icon representing the rider.
- **Functions:** Live ETA countdown, rider details (photo, name, vehicle type), and a quick-action button to call or message the rider directly without leaving the app.

## 2. Vendor Direct Messaging & Support Chat
**The Feature:** An integrated, real-time messaging system allowing customers to speak directly with vendors or Bexiemart support.
**The Flow:** 
- While viewing a product, the user has a question about sizing. They tap a floating **"Chat with Seller"** button.
- A sleek chat interface slides up. The user sends a text or uploads an image. 
- **Functions:** Real-time typing indicators, read receipts, voice note support, and the ability to link specific products directly in the chat window. For support, a chatbot can handle initial triage before routing to a human agent.

## 3. Comprehensive Reviews & Photo Ratings
**The Feature:** A user-generated content engine where buyers can leave detailed reviews with photos.
**The Flow:** 
- 24 hours after an order is marked "Delivered", the user gets a prompt: *"How was the Wireless Earbuds Pro?"*
- They are taken to a slick, minimalist review form. They tap 1-5 stars, write a quick comment, and upload a photo of the product they received.
- **Functions:** On the Product Details page, prospective buyers can now filter reviews by "With Photos" or sort by rating. This builds immense social proof and trust.

## 4. Curated Wishlists & Collections
**The Feature:** Upgrading the basic "Favorites" list into a robust collections system that can be shared.
**The Flow:** 
- The user taps the heart icon on a product. Instead of just saving it, a bottom sheet asks: *"Save to which collection?"*
- The user creates a new collection called "My Dream Tech Setup" or "Summer Wardrobe".
- **Functions:** Users can generate a public link to their collection and send it to friends or family (perfect for birthdays or weddings).

## 5. Advanced Search & Dynamic Filtering
**The Feature:** A powerful search engine overlay that helps users narrow down a massive catalog instantly.
**The Flow:** 
- Tapping the search bar on the Home screen opens a full-page overlay displaying **Recent Searches** and **Trending Categories**.
- After searching for "Sneakers", the user taps a **Filter** icon.
- A bottom sheet appears with: Price range sliders (e.g., GHS 50 - 200), brand checkboxes, size selectors, and sorting toggles (Price: Low to High, Most Popular).
- **Functions:** Instant, skeleton-loaded results that update live as filters are applied.

## 6. Loyalty Points (BexieCoins)
**The Feature:** A gamified rewards system to incentivize repeat purchases.
**The Flow:** 
- The user buys a GHS 100 item. A celebratory animation plays: *"You earned 10 BexieCoins!"*
- In their Wallet screen, a new "Rewards" card tracks their total points and VIP tier (Bronze, Silver, Gold).
- **Functions:** At checkout, a toggle switch allows them to *"Apply 50 BexieCoins for GHS 5.00 off."* This drastically increases customer retention.

## 7. Address Book & Multi-Location Management
**The Feature:** A dedicated management screen for delivery addresses.
**The Flow:** 
- The user navigates to `Profile > Delivery Addresses`.
- They tap **"Add New Location"**. A map opens for them to drop a pin accurately.
- They fill in the building name, apartment number, and assign a label ("Home", "Office", "Girlfriend's House").
- **Functions:** During the checkout flow, instead of typing an address, they simply horizontally swipe through their saved address cards and tap one to proceed.

## 8. Personalized AI Discovery
**The Feature:** An algorithmically driven recommendation engine.
**The Flow:** 
- As the user browses, the app silently tracks their category interests.
- The next time they open the app, the **"Just For You"** section on the Home screen is entirely populated with items tailored to their taste.
- **Functions:** Smart push notifications (e.g., *"We noticed you bought a phone. Need a case?"*). Cross-selling directly in the cart based on what others bought.
